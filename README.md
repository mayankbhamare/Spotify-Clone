This project uses **MediaPipe** for browser-based hand tracking. 
1. Click the **"Start Gesture"** button in the navigation bar.
2. Grant camera permissions.
3. **Volume Control**: Pinch your Thumb and Index finger. Move them apart to increase volume, and closer to decrease.
4. **Next Song**: Touch and hold your Thumb and Ring finger together for 3 seconds.
5. **Previous Song**: Touch and hold your Thumb and Middle finger together for 3 seconds.
6. **Play/Pause**: Touch and hold your Thumb and Pinky finger together for 3 seconds.
